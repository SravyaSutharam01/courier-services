Frequently Asked Questions


1.How can i track my shipment:

For tracking your shipment, please visit TRACK SHIPMENT and enter your tracking ID (AWB/Order ID/Mobile No.). Then, click on the 'SUBMIT' button to retrieve the Shipment


2.What payment methods do you accept

For COD shipment we do take payment by UPI Link and Cash. However we encourage payment through UPI Link to eliminate last minute hastle.


3.How can i change my delivery address:


As a delivery partner, we do not have the authority to change the delivery address, we request you to contact your merchant


4.Can i reschedule my date of delivery:

We cannot prepone delivery of your shipment due to operational limitations. But we can postpone shipment delivery date maximum by 5-7 days. Please reach out at customercare
